My name is Ben Newland, and I'm a freshman computer science major currently attending North Dakota State University. I am currently employed at Marcus Century Cinema in Fargo as a theater associate.

My goal for my career is to become a video game developer, although I am willing to pursue other options and opportunities in the field of computer science.

During my time at NDSU, I have been involved with the Bison Robotics team, specifically their NASA Lunabotics challenge team. I have also participated in band through the Gold Star Marching Band, where I perform on the drum line.
